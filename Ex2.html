Avocado Smash

Why Avocado Smash

Avocado smash is a quick, simple and healthy meal (or snack). It's quick to prepare, full of nutrients and good fats.
As a vegan, I'm always looking for a tasty dish and this meal has taste in abundance. Try it, you never know you might like it!!! .

Ingedients (for two)

  • 2 x avocados
  • 2 x ciabatta rolls
  • 100g of mushrooms,sliced
  • 2 x onions, chopped
  • 2 x table spoons of olive oil
  • 2 x garlic cloves
  • 1/2 a tea spoon of chilli flakes
  • A handfull of coriander

Preparation

  1. Peel and remove the stones from the Avocados, place them into a glass bowl and mash with a potato masher.
  2. Pour 2 table spoons of olive oil into a frying pan and place the pan on the stove on a low light
  3. Fry the until they start to caramelise
  4. Add the and fry for another 3 minutes
  5. Add the and fry for 1 minute
  6. Take off the heat and add to the bowl containing the mashed avocado
  7. With a gently stir the ingredients together
  8. While stirring add the chilli flakes and 2/3 of the coriander
  9. Place the 2 ciabatta under a hot grill for 1 minute
  10. Remove the ciabatta from the grill and place onto serving plates
  11. Spoon out the mixture (in equal amounts) onto the ciabatta
  12. Garnish with the remaining

Enjoy!!
